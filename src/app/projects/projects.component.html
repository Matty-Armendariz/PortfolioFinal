Ultra Connect 4:

Overview: Created an AI to play a version of connect 4 in which the game does not end till you fill up the entire board and the score is continuously tallied by parsing the board and updating the score after every move. This AI implements the minimax algorithm from game theory to look look up to look a certain amount of moves in the future and determine the best possible to optimize the odds of winning

What I Learned: I learned a lot about several different types of game theory algorithms and how to implement them such as expectimax, montecarlo tree search and, nash equilibrium algorithms. Finally landing on the minimax to implement in this project. To drastically improve the run time and efficiency of the project I implemented alpha beta pruning to systematically remove unecessary permutation calculations. This results in an AI that can look 8 moves ahead (including all relevant permutations of possible moves) in around 2 seconds, and 10-11 moves in the future in 10-15 seconds

What I Would Improve: As far as the algorithm, I cannot think of any way to further improve the AI. However, if I where to spend more time on the project I could definately use a game library like pygame to make a much more aesthetically pleasing product.
